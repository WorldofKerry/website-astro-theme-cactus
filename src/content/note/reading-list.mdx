## In Progress

- _How to Win Friends and Influence People in the Digital Age_ by Dale Carnegie

  I don't think I'm all that great in social contexts, and thus am looking to improve. Getting better at remembering people's names is a tip that I really want to get better at.

- _Unreasonable Hospitality_ by Will Guidara

  Cool take on how hospitality can affect people outside the industry — lessons about how we treat people in general. Really interesting restaurant stories, too.

## Completed

Additional entries via [blog posts](/tags/books/)!

- _The Joy of Costco: A Treasure Hunt from A to Z_ by David & Susan Schwartz

  A super cool, coffee-table-worthy book. I'm a big fan of logistics, and understanding everything Costco does to make their business model work is fascinating. Great history lesson at the beginning.

- _Nuclear War: A Scenario_ by Annie Jacobsen\_

  An in-depth look at protocol and decision-making in the event of nuclear war — covers succession, responsibilities, and contingency planning.
